The Idea

"Semi-automated hydroponics systems on building rooftops."

Looking for engineers, scientist, designers & project managers to make this vision a reality.

The Approach

Design variables:

  • Root distance
  • Water flow in gutter
  • Gutter base profile
  • Gutter width
  • Gutter angle
  • Distance from light

Control variables:

  • Light spectrum
  • Light intensity
  • Air temperature
  • Air flow rate
  • Nutrient concentration
  • Water temperature

Sensors:

  • EC solution
  • PH solution
  • Root camera
  • Leaf camera
  • Ultrasound

Constraints:

  • Light must be provided by LEDS
  • No intervention until harvest
  • Visually appealing and silent
  • Modular and fast setup

Cost Function:

  • Plant yield
  • Plant quality
  • Energy use
  • Ressource consumption
  • Total volume
  • Growth time
  • Plant death
  • Capital cost
  • Maintenance cost
